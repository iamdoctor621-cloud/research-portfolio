## Active Research Projects

### 1. GLP-1 Receptor Agonist Prescribing Trends in Neurodegenerative Disease
- **Platform:** TriNetX
- **Design:** Retrospective serial cross-sectional study (2018-2025)
- **Population:** 2.29M+ patients with neurodegenerative conditions
- **Methods:** ICD-10 cohort building, temporal trend analysis
- **Target Journals:** JAMA Network Open, Neurology Clinical Practice, Pharmacoepidemiology & Drug Safety
- **Status:** Analysis complete; manuscript in preparation

### 2. PCSK9 Inhibitor Outcomes in High-Risk Cardiovascular Populations
- **Platform:** TriNetX
- **Design:** Retrospective cohort study with propensity score matching (PSM)
- **Focus:** MACE outcomes, racial disparities (Black/African American), dose-response analysis
- **Methods:** 1:1 PSM, Cox proportional hazards regression
- **Status:** Subgroup analyses ongoing

### 3. Physical Activity Trajectories and Neurological Outcomes
- **Platform:** All of Us Research Program (Controlled Tier)
- **Design:** Retrospective cohort study
- **Focus:** Longitudinal physical activity patterns and incident neurological disease
- **Status:** Cohort definition complete; analysis in progress

### 4. Prodromal Non-Motor Symptom Trajectories in Parkinson's Disease
- **Platform:** All of Us Research Program
- **Design:** Retrospective case-control study
- **Focus:** Early non-motor symptom burden preceding Parkinson's diagnosis
- **Status:** Manuscript in preparation
